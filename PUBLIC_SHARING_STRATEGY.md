# Public Sharing Strategy

## Public

- Project overview.
- One-page brief.
- Demo video link.
- Screenshots.
- Summary-level results.
- Current limitations.
- PhD research direction.

## Private

- Full code.
- Raw experiment data.
- Raw human labels.
- Private conversation and drafting history.
- Application drafts.
- Contact strategy and outreach drafts.

## Reason

The public repository provides a verifiable demonstration while reducing plagiarism and premature disclosure risk during the PhD application stage.

## Authorship

Keep local commit, tag, and release timestamps for authorship traceability. The full private repository can be shared selectively with academic reviewers upon request.
